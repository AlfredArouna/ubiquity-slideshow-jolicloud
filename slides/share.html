Share and Socialize with Friends

  • You can share everything on Jolicloud with your friends by using the built-in Stream. Check it out and see what your friends are up to, and even what apps they're using.
  • Joli OS also has a built-in Guest Mode, so you can also share your computer with your friends and family. Plus, you can use this feature to login to any Joli OS computer in the world and instantly access your desktop, as if it was your own.
